Good web reads

I send people to these places whenever they have questions about learning design, programming, or being generally awesome.

  • Butterick’s Practical Typography Some people preach that Comic Sans is bad. Others actually teach you typography—enter Butterick.
  • Farnam Street Go to bed smarter than you woke up.
  • Humble Pied Super short video interviews with really smart people.
  • Quotes and Accents Jessica Hische lays the law on proper quotes and accents.
  • Open Culture Life isn’t only about pixels and code. Get cultured.
  • Public Speaking advice on Delicious My personal collection on presentation-giving tips saved over the years.
  • Signal v. Noise A publication about business, design, programming, writing, and the web by Basecamp since 1999.
  • The Distance Stories about hard-working, long-lasting businesses and the people behind them. Brought to you by Basecamp.
  • Superhero.js A curated collection of articles to get better at JavaScript.
  • Typography for Lawyers Rock-solid how-to on typography, for everyone.
