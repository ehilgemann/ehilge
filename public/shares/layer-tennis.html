Out of Layer Tennis Retirement

I’ve been keeping my design skills sharp during the offseason to once again step foot on the Layer Tennis courts.

Shared on August 08, 2014

After a few years of endlessly boring Fridays, the fine folks at Coudal and Adobe have teamed up once again to bring us Layer Tennis. With rookie design prospects looking to break onto the #LYT4 big leagues along side veteran creative powerhouses, this season’s Layer Tennis looks to pick up right where we left off.

And what better way to kick it off than a friendly exhibition match as I play against New York’s Jason Koxvold, with play-by-play commentary from one of my favorite writers, Alissa Walker.

The first match starts today at 11:30am. Root for me. Or don’t—either way, enjoy the show.
