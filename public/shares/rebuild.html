I’m speaking at re:build

I took a break from speaking, but I’m hopping back in the saddle in Indianapolis for re:build.

Shared on April 09, 2014

The kind folks who put on re:build invited me to speak at this year’s conference. I’ll be talking about “All the Wrong Things,” exposing lessons and ah-ha moments as a web-maker I’ve learned at Threadless and Basecamp the hard way. If you’re in the Midwest, snag a ticket and come to Indy—they’re only $149.

There’s a mighty fine roster waiting to hang with you, too, including Ms. @nrrrdcore herself, and the incomparable @rands. Guaranteed amounts of fun, tomfoolery, and maybe some inspiration, too. See you there.
